Autonomous Multi-Agent Reconstruction of Prebiotic Reaction Networks Reveals Organizational Features of Amino Acid Synthesis

ASTRA is an autonomous multi-agent system that reconstructs prebiotic chemical reaction networks — uncovering the organizational principles behind how amino acids are synthesized.
